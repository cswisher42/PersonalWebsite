Introduction

Greetings, my name is Chuck Swisher and I am from Kansas City, Kansas. As a child (at least more of one than I am now), I always found myself most interested in science fiction movies and books than any other genre. I believe that a large part of who I am can be attributed to those books and movies because the feats of technology displayed made me wish to be one of the people who brought their made up gadgets into the real world. As I continued to grow, computers caught my attention and I soon started telling my parents and others how to operate their different pieces of technology, as I am sure it was/is for most computer science majors. I quickly discovered that I liked working with computers and their many different applications and I was decided that I would work with them.

Why I Chose K-State

Fast forward to the end of high school and I now was in a position to make my first real, meaningful decision about how to pursue my goal. As the process of school selection began it was readily apparent that Kansas State's College of Engineering had a reputation of producing work ready engineers and having a high return on investment. Being an adequate distance from home and having quality scholarships available it was not a very hard decision to select my destination of Manhattan. After hearing about the Scholars program and all of the clubs and activities available, I was more than certain that I wanted to come to K-State. Additionally, several of my good friends from high school are here at K-State as well. My not having to completely start over as far as friends and acquaintances are concerned was a nice added bonus to the deal.

After spending all of my money and time there, I became friends with the workers and owners of the store.
